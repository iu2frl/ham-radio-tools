Stub Configuration Quick Reference

Practical Build with T Connector (SO-239):

• Connect Radio → T connector → Antenna (two axial ports)
• Connect stub to the perpendicular T port (shunt configuration)
• Use short-circuit (both ends connected) or open-ended (one end floating) as needed

Notch Filter (Reject): Short-circuited stub in shunt → blocks center frequency, passes all others

Bandpass Filter (Pass): Short-circuited stub in shunt → passes center frequency ± BW, rejects outside

⚠️ Short-circuited: Both ends connected (more robust, weatherproof)
⚠️ Open-ended: One end floating (resonant at specific frequency, can collect moisture)
